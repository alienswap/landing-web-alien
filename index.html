What is Staking?

Learn what staking is, how to do it with Alien Swap, and what you can expect.

1

Deposit

Staking begins by depositing your tokens into a pool. When you deposit, they are moved from your wallet and safely stored in a "vault." This is boiled down to providing liquidity to receive rewards. If you stake, you are lending a single asset. Through farming, you're creating liquidity tokens with equal amounts of BNB and Token.

2

Earn, Harvest and Compound

As long as your tokens remain in the vault, you will earn assets that can be claimed through "harvesting." The rewards are then sent to your wallet, and you can then use them, sell them, or "compound" them. Compounding them by re-entering them into the pool allows you to have more overall tokens that will be earning money for you.

3

Exit and Withdraw

You can exit your pools at any time and do anything with your tokens and rewards. This ends your ability to continue earning an APY% in the pool, but enables you to explore OTHER pools, or make use of your tokens. If you don't want to exit, you can simply just withdraw the rewards while leaving your staked tokens to continue earning.
